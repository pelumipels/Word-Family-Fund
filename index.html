About Word Family Fund

Word Family Fund is a venture studio with the mandate to strategically mentor and equip tech entrepreneurs with the required resources to bring their products and projects to a point of sustainable growth.

The venture studio would invest and cobuild with entrepreneurs in Africa. They would also be mentored through the product development life cycle.
